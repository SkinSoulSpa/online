Imagery & Photography Direction

The "Misty Dream" Aesthetic

Photography should feel intimate, tactile, and quiet. Avoid overly commercial, high-contrast, or clinical spa imagery. We want to capture the feeling of the treatment, not just the action.

Soft Focus Macro Texture Natural Light Ethereal Warmth

Proposed Image Color Palette:

Images should be color-graded to match these tones, desaturating bright blues/reds and enhancing warm neutrals.

Do

  • Use shallow depth of field (blurred backgrounds).
  • Focus on details: hands, water droplets, fabric texture, steam.
  • Use warm, diffuse natural light.
  • Include "negative space" for text overlays.
  • Crop images into organic shapes where possible.

Don't

  • Use harsh flash or direct sunlight.
  • Show clinical tools or machines prominently.
  • Use perfectly posed, smiling models looking at camera.
  • Use cool, blue-toned clinical white.
  • Oversaturate colors.
