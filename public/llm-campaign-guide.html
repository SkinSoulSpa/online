Campaign Creation with LLMs

A guide to crafting soulful, on-brand content using AI assistance.

The Process

  1. Feed your LLM: Provide comprehensive information about Skin Soul Spa (website, images, campaign ideas). Recommended tools: Google Notebook and Grok.
  2. Initial Creation: Ask it to create content for your campaign idea once it understands the business context.
  3. Filter the Generic: Be aware that initial outputs are often generic ("cookie-cutter").
  4. Curate: Identify the strong elements and discard the rest.
  5. Iterate & Expand: Highlight the good parts and ask the LLM to expand on them—propose options or "crank up the creativity."
  6. Refine: Polish the final output. Remove fluff and ensure conciseness.
